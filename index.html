Welcome to CowCare Hub

CowCare Hub is a complete informational student project created using HTML. It provides knowledge about cow care, indigenous cow breeds, common cow diseases, and the importance of cows in Indian agriculture and culture.

Our mission is to spread awareness about animal welfare, ethical cow care and sustainable farming practices.

About CowCare Hub

CowCare Hub promotes cow protection and humane treatment. Cows play a vital role in milk production, farming and rural economy.

  • Awareness about cow welfare
  • Support for farmers
  • Protection of indigenous cow breeds

Proper Cow Care

Proper cow care ensures good health, higher milk production and longer life for cows.

  • Clean and dry shelter
  • Fresh fodder and water
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Kind and humane treatment
